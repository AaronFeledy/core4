  - A per-entry ZIP inflate cap is insufficient — entries each under the cap can cumulatively exhaust memory; thread a running decompressed-bytes budget across stored AND deflated entries.
  - The Landofile parser had TWO independent recursion families: block indentation recursion (bounded by line count) and inline-array recursion (`parseScalar`→`parseInlineArray`) which is line-local and was previously unbounded; depth caps must cover both.
  - `DecompressionStream` output is capped by counting chunk bytes in the reader loop and calling `reader.cancel()` on overflow — works for both `gzip` and `zstd` in Bun, and keeps the zst/gz code path shared.
